Where Every Dollar Goes

Every donation, minus Stripe and Wise transfer fees, goes directly to Bico Duro and the kids. TrueSight DAO takes no cut.

The Flow

  1. You donate via Stripe Checkout on this site
  2. Stripe deducts their processing fee (2.9% + $0.30 per transaction)
  3. The remaining amount is recorded in the TrueSight DAO treasury-cache ledger
  4. Funds are transferred to Bico Duro via Wise to Brazil (0.5% + $1.50 fee)
  5. Bico Duro receives the final amount via Pix (Brazilian instant payment)
  6. Every transfer is published on the public transparency dashboard at tribomirimbahia.truesight.me
