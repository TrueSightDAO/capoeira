Where the Cacao Grows

Capoeira and cacao share the same soil, the same hands, the same community. Mestre Bico Duro walks us through Fazenda Jesus de Deus, the Bahian farm whose beans become Agroverse's ceremonial cacao and chocolate — and whose sales fund this capoeira program.

The Loop

Every chocolate bar and every block of ceremonial cacao you buy from Agroverse is one cacao tree planted in Bahia, one unit of revenue routed through transparent ledgers, and one direct contribution to the Tribo Mirim after-school program.
